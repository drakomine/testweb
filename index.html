Useful Commands

Navigate, manage your team, and control your economy with these essential commands.

Economy & General

  • /balance – Check your current in-game currency.
  • /pay <player> <amount> – Transfer money to another player.
  • /toggletp – Enable or disable teleport requests from other players.
  • /backpack – Access your portable extra inventory.
  • /shops – View a list of available player shops.
  • /pvp on – Activate Player vs. Player combat for yourself.
  • /pvp off – Deactivate Player vs. Player combat for yourself.
  • /qs find <item> – Find item in shop.

Navigation & Teleportation

  • /warp spawn – Instantly return to the server spawn point.
  • /sethome – Designate your personal home location.
  • /home – Teleport directly back to your designated home.
  • /tpa <player> – Send a request to teleport to another player.
  • /tpahere <player> – Ask a player to teleport to your location.
  • /tpaccept / /tpdeny – Respond to incoming teleport requests.
  • /lobby – Return to the central server hub.

Land Claim Management

  • /claim – Secure the land chunk you are currently standing on.
  • /unclaim – Release a previously claimed land chunk.
  • /trust <player> – Grant building and access permissions to a player on your claimed land.
  • /untrust <player> – Revoke building and access permissions from a player.
  • /claimslist – View a comprehensive list of all your owned claimed lands.
  • /trustlist – View a comprehensive list of all players you have given access to your land claims.

Team System Operations

  • /team create <name> – Establish your own new team.
  • /team invite <player> – Extend an invitation for a player to join your team.
  • /team join <name> – Accept an invitation to become a member of a team.
  • /team kick <player> – Remove a player from your team.
  • /team leave – Voluntarily depart from your current team.
  • /team promote <player> – Designate another team member as a leader.
  • /team chat – Engage in private conversation exclusively with your team members.

Player Shops

How to Create Your Shop

1. Place a chest at your desired shop location.

2. Left-click the chest to open the shop creation interface.

3. Follow the on-screen prompts to set your selling item(s) and their prices.

How to Buy/Sell from a Shop

PC: Simply Left-click the chest.

Mobile: Press the signboard above the chest.

Effortless Shop Relocation

PC: Shift + Right-Click to gently pick up your villager.

Bedrock: Sneak + Interact to gently pick up your villager.

Place it anywhere else and effortlessly reconfigure your shop as needed.
